All About

S. Raj Kumari is founder and Roja Patnaik is co-founder of "Visakhapatnam Blood/Plasma Doners Club".

We in our club believes togetherness is the power. We focus on creating and expanding our volunteer count to extend our help for people in need.

We are a non profit community and our main objective is to make sure that everything is done to vulnerable persons.

Our ultimate goal is to provide fast, efficient, and reliable way to get life saving blood, totally Free of cost.
